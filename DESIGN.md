# Harmony Design Guide

> Photo Consent Version 2.1 “UI Refresh”  
> “Harmony” is the private project name shared by the creators.

## Logo

The final interface logo is a camera outline with a circular lens and leaf sprig. It does not use a heart. The mark uses a soft teal-to-leaf gradient.

## Choice colors

1. Green — Photo OK
2. Yellow — Face not visible
3. Orange — Group photo
4. Light red — Blurred or partial body
5. Red — Skip photography

Purple and face-expression icons are not used. Colors support scanning only and do not rank the participant’s choices.

## Writing tone

Gentle, direct, non-judgmental, and easy to understand. “どの選択肢を選んでも大丈夫です。” is a core product principle.

## Motion

Motion should be calm and short. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
